About Me

I am a creative UX designer with six years of experience between startups, independent consulting, agencies, and corporations.

My goal is to help people and businesses by validating ideas with prototypes, advancing processes, and fostering collaboration. I believe in putting users first, testing assumptions, and making software accessible.

When I’m not designing, I like to spend time making music, hiking in the mountains of Colorado, and creating content for my Pokémon TCG newsletter.
